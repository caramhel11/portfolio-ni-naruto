Transformations

Forms &
Power Levels

Each form represents a new chapter in Naruto's growth — from a boy who couldn't clone himself to a warrior capable of rivalling gods.

01
Base Naruto
Genin → Chunin → Jonin

Raw talent hidden behind a knucklehead attitude. Mastered the Shadow Clone Jutsu and Rasengan where most give up. Foundation of everything that follows.

02
Sage Mode
Mount Myōboku Training

Trained by the toads of Mount Myōboku, Naruto learns to absorb natural energy. Sage Mode amplifies his speed, strength, and perception beyond normal limits.

03
Kurama Chakra Mode
Partnership with Kurama

After breaking through hatred and forging a bond with the Nine-Tailed Fox, Naruto wields Kurama's golden chakra freely — clad in a radiant shroud.

04
Tailed Beast Mode
Full Bijuu Manifestation

Naruto and Kurama become one — manifesting as a colossal Nine-Tailed Fox. A single Bijuudama reshapes the landscape. Fought toe-to-toe with the Ten-Tails.

05
Six Paths Sage Mode
Granted by Hagoromo Ōtsutsuki

The Sage of Six Paths entrusts Naruto with divine chakra. Truth-Seeking Balls orbit him. He can fly. He saved the world in this form — twice.

06
Baryon Mode
Final Form · Life-Burning

The absolute limit. Naruto and Kurama's chakra fuse like nuclear matter — consuming both their lives. Used to defeat Isshiki Ōtsutsuki. The price: Kurama.
